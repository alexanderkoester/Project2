Marker

These versatile paint markers will work on surfaces like paper, glass, rocks, wood, metal, plastic, and more. No need to get separate glass paint or paint for ceramics, these markers can do it all. Each marker features a chisel tip and a rounded tip so that you can create both wide and thin lines while drawing. These pens are filled with acrylic paint that's opaque and acid-free for the best non-fading surface coverage. You won't have to worry about waiting a long time for your artwork to be ready due to the quick-drying formula. Enjoy all the fun of painting without the mess of getting water cups or paintbrushes.
