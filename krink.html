Krink

Krink products are shipping daily from a headquarters in Brooklyn to everywhere from California to Moscow to Tokyo. The product line has grown to offer a number of different markers and inks that are unique to the market in their style, history and quality. The trademark paint-drip aesthetic has found its way into art and design becoming a standard for street-inspired artists on a global level. It's been 15 years and what started as a product created to fit the specific needs of graffiti writers has grown into a product line with a range of creative tools for creative thinkers. Watch closely over the next 15 as Krink continues to build its name as the premier art supply line for a new generation of artists.

K-11

Multi-Surface Versatility - Krink's K-11 acrylic paint pens have a 3mm tip that glides smoothly across plastic, paper, canvas and painted surfaces; it's ideal for detailed illustrations or paintings. Bold & Dynamic Paint - K-11 opaque acrylic paint markers have vibrant, water-based acrylic paint, ensuring your art pieces and designs stand out. Precision Control Tip - K-11 acrylic graffiti markers were created with precision in mind; each art pen has a small valve-action tip for thin lines and magnificent control over Krinks artist-grade paint.
